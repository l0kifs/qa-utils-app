Task Estimation Tool

The Task Estimation Tool helps you estimate the complexity and time required to complete tasks based on several key factors:

  • Effort: How much work is involved (e.g., time, resources).
    • Example:
    • 1: Simple task.
    • 5: Multi-step task with a lot of manual work.
  • Clarity: How clear the requirements are.
    • Example:
    • 1: Clear requirements, no questions.
    • 5: Poorly defined task, requires research.
  • Technical Risks: Potential technical challenges.
    • Example:
    • 1: Minimal or no risks.
    • 5: High risks, requires approvals and backup solutions.
  • Team Experience: Team familiarity with similar tasks.
    • Example:
    • 1: Task is within the team's expertise.
    • 5: Team is facing a new domain.
  • Communication: Level of collaboration required with other parties.
    • Example:
    • 1: Minimal communication required to resolve the task.
    • 5: Task requires constant synchronization with many parties.
  • Risk Level: General risk associated with the task.

Enter the details for each factor in the form below to get an estimated complexity score and time for the task.
